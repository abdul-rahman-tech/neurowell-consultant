Welcome 👋

Mental health is just as important as physical health. You've taken the right step by being here. Let us guide you towards a healthier mind.

🧠

What Does a Psychiatrist Do?

A psychiatrist is a medical doctor specializing in mental health. They diagnose, treat, and prevent mental, emotional, and behavioral disorders. Unlike psychologists, they can prescribe medications and provide therapy.

⚠️

Common Symptoms to Watch

  • Persistent sadness or hopelessness
  • Excessive anxiety or worry
  • Sudden mood swings
  • Difficulty concentrating or sleeping
  • Withdrawal from social activities
  • Unexplained physical symptoms
🛡️

Precautions & Self-Care

  • Never skip prescribed medication
  • Maintain regular sleep schedules
  • Avoid alcohol and substance use
  • Talk to someone you trust
  • Practice mindfulness daily
  • Attend all follow-up sessions
💊

Common Conditions We Treat

  • Depression & Mood Disorders
  • Anxiety & Panic Disorders
  • Bipolar Disorder
  • Schizophrenia
  • OCD & PTSD
  • ADHD & Eating Disorders
🤝

When to Seek Help

Seek professional help when symptoms last more than 2 weeks, interfere with daily functioning, or when you feel overwhelmed. Early intervention leads to better outcomes. There is no shame in seeking help — it's a sign of strength.
